japanese

I really like Japanese Culture, and I wanted to learn a new language, but a different one, one that would be more challenging, so naturally I started learning Japanese. All of this started around 2 years ago, and I've been doing it by myself, whenever I can, lately I've been trying to study it everyday, even if its only 30 minutes a day. Honestly I am proud of my progress so far, I would say I am at least JLPT N4 level, I can read hiragana an katakana without any problem, although Kanji has been hard, as expected, but I am trying my best to memorize and learn it, and now I can sometimes understand the meaning of them from looking at the radicals.
